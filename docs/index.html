Sarah is a first-year Health Informatics student. On a typical Wednesday she has lectures until 4pm, a part-time shift until 8pm, and an assignment due Thursday morning.

She has €35 for the week and a shared dorm kitchen she can use between 9 and 11pm if nobody else has claimed it.

She knows what a balanced diet looks like. She is literally studying it. But knowing that she needs 50g of protein and knowing what to buy at Lidl for €35 that actually delivers that — while reusing ingredients across the week so nothing goes to waste — are two completely different problems.

She eats whatever is fast. Not because she does not care. Because the planning itself costs time she does not have.

NutriBudget solves exactly that gap. Not nutrition education — she has that. Nutritional execution under real constraints.

Your budget, your equipment, your leftovers. A full week planned in seconds.

Installation

Download the binary for your system below, then make it executable:

chmod +x nutribudget-x86_64
./nutribudget-x86_64 --budget 35 --diet vegetarian --equipment shared-dorm-kitchen --time 20

Or build from source if you have Rust installed:

git clone https://github.com/NamazziJosephine/nutribudget
cd nutribudget
cargo build --release
./target/release/nutribudget --budget 35 --diet vegetarian --equipment shared-dorm-kitchen --time 20
